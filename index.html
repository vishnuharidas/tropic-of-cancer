Tropics of Cancer and Capricorn

❓

This map shows the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn.

Tropic of Cancer (red line)

Tropic of Cancer (23.43663°N) is the northernmost latitude where the sun can be directly overhead, occurring at the June solstice (mostly on June 21st).

Tropic of Capricorn (blue line)

Tropic of Capricorn (-23.43663°S) is the southernmost latitude where the sun can be directly overhead, occurring at the December solstice (mostly on December 21st).

Subsolar Point (black dashed line)

The black dashed line represents the current subsolar point based on today's date. The subsolar point is the latitude where the sun is directly overhead at noon on a given day.
